About SQT

SQT Web Solutions aim to be one of the leading technology companies based in Africa providing web services and application development to our global customer. In order to achieve this, we vow to put in our possible best in every project we handle. We understand that customer satisfaction plays a crucial role in attaining our target goal, therefore we made it our priority to deliver projects that will put a smile on our clients faces.
